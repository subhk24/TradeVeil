Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity for products?
Minimum order quantities vary by product and are specified on the bulk order management page.
How can I request a custom quotation?
You can request a quotation using the "Request Quotation" feature in your dashboard or the quotation page.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and other payment methods as agreed upon with our customers.
How do I track my order?
Use the "Order Tracking" feature in your dashboard or the order tracking page to check your order status.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Order modifications or cancellations are subject to approval and must be requested promptly via customer support.
Do you offer discounts for bulk purchases?
Yes, we offer pricing tiers and discounts based on order volume. Contact us for details.
